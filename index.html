About Us

We specialize in prevention, diagnostic and treatment products in healthcare, diversifying in construction and other general supplies. We offer complete sourcing, supply chain, and 4PL solutions including: vendor prequalification and audits, dossier reviews, product sampling and quality testing, forecasting, demand planning, last-mile logistics, real-time shipment tracking, and storage and warehousing. Bigskys Solutions Ltd is a Kenyan organization with offices in the Kiambu County with enough staff to handle deadlines.

Mission

Bigskys Solutions Ltd mission is to strengthen, develop, and manage secure, reliable, cost-effective, and sustainable supply chains to improve healthcare through increased access to quality and affordable products. We achieve this by:
• Providing quality, best-value products and end-to-end supply chain services: Plan, Source, Move, Track, Store and Quality.
• Deploying innovative, data-driven solutions to enhance supply chain agility.
